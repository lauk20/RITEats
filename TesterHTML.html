    Learn all about the features of RIT Dining. From meal plan options to adding funds to nutrition. Find tools and resources that will help you adjust to dining at RIT.

  • Dining Questions? Contact the RIT Service Center

    For dining questions, such as meal plans, Tiger Bucks and vending machines, contact the RIT Service Center at help.rit.edu or 585-475-5000.

    Learn More
  • We are Cashless

    RIT Dining is cashless. Securely add money to a dining account through Tiger Spend or at a Tiger Spend reload station. Visitors may purchase a reload card and add funds via cash or card.
